Adopt a dog!

Golden Retriever

One of the most popular breeds in the United States, golden retrievers are widely known for their kind and playful temperament. Due to their hard-working nature, they are a popular choice of breed for service dogs.

Shiba Inu

Once a breed of hunting dog, the Shiba Inu is a breed known today as a great companion dog. Shibas make great watch dogs and have relatively high needs for mental stimulation.

Siberian Husky

As a pack dog, huskies are a very sociable breed, generally getting along well with both other dogs and humans. Bred for pulling sleds through frozen expanses, they are a very energetic type of companion that require ample space for running around.

Shih Tzu

Incredibly fluffy and tiny, these bundles of fluff make great lapdogs. The shih tzu is known to get along well with children and are highly adaptable to all sorts of indoor living environments.

Australian Shepherd

The Australian Shepherd as a tough and active breed born for herding ranch animals. Not only are they incredibly energetic, they are also super intelligent. In the domestic setting, they are even known to attempt to herd children.

Irish Terrier

The Irish Terrier is a medium-sized dog that is both a hard-working breed and an affectionate companion at home. As a result of their protective nature towards their owners, they do not typically get along with other dogs.
