Adopt a cat!

Tabby

A tabby cat is one of the most unique domestic breeds with a distinctive M-shaped marking on its forehead; stripes by its eyes and across its cheeks, along its back, and around its legs and tail.

Siamese

The carefully refined, more extreme-featured, modern-style Siamese is characterised by blue almond-shaped eyes; a triangular head shape; large ears; an elongated, slender, and muscular body; and various forms of point colouration.

Ragdoll

This furry ragdoll is known for being incredibly affectionate and easily attachable. They are docile, gentile, and sweet, and will be very dependent on your attention.

Bombay

This bombay cat has short, glossy black hair with a muscular build and copper-gold eyes. This little fellow is known to have a wild nature and can be a bit bossy, but they are a social butterfly and very smart.

Burmese

This little burmese kitten will grow to be a medium-sized feline with a strong, muscular body, a round head, and round paws. They are extremely friendly, trusting, and playful.

Maine Coon

One of the oldest natural breeds in North America, this maine coon is sweet natured and playful , and has a well-proportioned and balanced structure despite its size.
